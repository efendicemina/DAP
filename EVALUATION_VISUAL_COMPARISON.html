Interpretation

  • The first major jump came from moving from plain TF-IDF retrieval to embedding-based retrieval.
  • The second major jump came from v5, where the retrieval became smarter because of better metadata and richer routing logic.
  • The largest overall gain is in Top-1 Accuracy and MRR, which means the system is much better at putting the correct source first, not only somewhere in the top results.
  • The increase in Top-5 Recall shows the final pipeline is also better at keeping relevant content within the candidate set.

Short Conclusion

The progression is:

v4 TF-IDF -> v4 Embeddings -> v5 Embeddings

and the final v5 version is clearly the strongest among the evaluated retrieval variants.

Best One-Line Summary for Report

The project evolved from a lexical TF-IDF retrieval baseline to an embedding-based FAISS pipeline with richer metadata, and the evaluation shows a clear improvement in all key metrics, especially Top-1 Accuracy and MRR.
